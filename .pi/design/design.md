# Design Conventions

## Preview
- Screens are viewable at `/p/<screen-name>`
- Example: `/p/login` shows the login screen

## Style: Lyra
- Boxy and sharp
- Use `rounded-none` or `rounded-sm` (not rounded-lg)
- Borders: `border border-zinc-200` or `border-zinc-800`
- No shadows or minimal `shadow-none`
- Monospace font: `font-mono`

## Typography
- Font: `font-mono` (JetBrains Mono)
- Sizes: `text-sm` `text-base` `text-lg` `text-2xl`
- Headings: `font-semibold`
- Body: `text-zinc-600`

## Spacing
- Use: `p-2` `p-4` `p-6` `p-8`
- Gap: `gap-2` `gap-4` `gap-6`

## Colors (Neutral)
- Backgrounds: `bg-white` `bg-zinc-950` `bg-zinc-900`
- Text: `text-zinc-950` `text-zinc-600` `text-zinc-400`
- Accent: `text-zinc-950` `bg-zinc-950` (dark primary)
- Borders: `border-zinc-200` `border-zinc-800`

## Icons
- Use Phosphor icons or Lucide (both work)

## Rules
- No gradients
- No rounded corners (sharp edges)
- No shadows
- Monospace typography
- Neutral/monochrome palette
